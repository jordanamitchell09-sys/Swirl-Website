Basic Outline of the Filmmaking Process

No matter what you're creating (movie, tv show, short film) the steps are all the same.

Pre-Production

After you've brainstormed and have you're idea, you will start pre-production. Here, you will plan out all the logistics for your project like: the budget, schedule, cast & crew, and of course the script.

Your role on the roject will determine what you'll be doing during this phase

You'll need to go through your script and take note of every character, set, and prop. Then you will need to figure out a budget that will fit the project, or change some things in the script to make it less exspensive
